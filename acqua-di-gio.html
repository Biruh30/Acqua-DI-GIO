Our Heritage

A Story Born
from the Sea

Acqua di Giò was born from Giorgio Armani's personal journey to Pantelleria, a volcanic island between Sicily and Tunisia. Captivated by the island's raw beauty — the black lava rock meeting crystalline sea — he sought to capture that freedom in a bottle.

Thirty years on, Acqua di Giò remains one of the world's most beloved fragrances — a testament to the enduring power of nature's simplicity and the artistry of Italian perfumery.

  • Crafted in Grasse, the perfume capital of the world
  • Certified sustainable sourcing for all natural ingredients
  • Awarded "Fragrance of the Decade" — Fragrance Foundation
  • Distributed in over 120 countries across 6 continents
  • Refillable bottles program since 2020 — reducing waste by 40%
